<4.3 평화공원>

제주특별자치도 제주시 명랑로 430 (봉개동)


제주 4.3평화공원은 4.3사건으로인한 제주도 민간인 학살을 기억하고 추념하며, 화해와 평화의 미래를 열어가기 위한 평화 인권기념공원이다. 제주4·3평화기념관, 위령제단, 추념광장, 행방불명인표석, 위령탑 등이 자리하고 있으며 평화기념관 같은 경우에는 4.3 사건의 시작부터 종결, 그 이후의 사건들까지 연도에 따라 전시와 설명이 되어있다.


<제주 4.3 사건>

제주 4.3사건이란 1947년 3월 1일 경찰의 발포사건을 기점으로 하여, 1948년 4월 3일 남한만의 단독정부 수립에 반대한 남로당 제주도당의 무장봉기와 미 군정의 강압이 계기가 되어 제주도에서 일어난 민중항쟁


<4.3 Peace Park>

430 Myungrangro Jejusi, Jeju (Bonggaedong)


Jeju 4.3 Peace Park is a Peace Human Rights Memorial Park that was created to commemorate and remember the massacre of Jeju civilians due to an incident in 1947, thus an uprising in response which began on April 3, thus the 4.3 incident, and to open a future of reconciliation and peace. The Jeju 4.3 Peace Memorial Hall, Memorial Altar, Honouring Square, Missing People Stone Post, and Memorial Top are located there. The Jeju 4.3 Peace Memorial Hall contains exhibits with explanations from the beginning of the 4.3 incident to the end, displayed according to the year.


<The Jeju 4.3 incident>

The Jeju 4.3 incident was a Democracy Movement even which happened in Jeju in 1947. It presents a Police shooting case as the starting point, and Namro Party’s armed rising opposed to South Korea’s single government (1948.4.3) and America’s pressure as the response. The full extent of the event, including the numbers imprisoned and killed, was not made known until after the Democracy Movement of 1987, over 40 years later.
