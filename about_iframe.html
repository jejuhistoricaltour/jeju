Welcome to ‘Jeju Historical Tour’ website. This website is created by Minjeong Byeon, as an output of her personal project who is currently attending Branksome Hall Asia in Jeju.

The website is produced for a purpose of helping English and Korean tourists who are interested in exploring historical sites in Jeju. There is a total of 20 places that are presented in the website with descriptions in both languages and pictures. Those places are focused on mainly historical sites, but also includes few of must-go places in Jeju.

This project has been running for 6 months and now it is finally presented to you. Hope this site helps you with your trip to Jeju. If you have any questions, comments, or anything to say, feel free to share your opinions via Q&A page.


Thank you for visiting.

Sincerely,

Minjeong Byeon
